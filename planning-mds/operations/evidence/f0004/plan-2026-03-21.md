## Key Decisions Made

1. **Mental model:** "My Work" (default) + "Assigned By Me" (manager/admin only). No "Team Queue" or "All In Scope" in Phase 1.
2. **Assignment:** DistributionManager and Admin can assign to any active internal user. Other roles self-assign only.
3. **Visibility:** Ownership-based (assignee or creator), not region-based.
4. **Status changes:** Assignee-only. Managers cannot complete tasks on behalf of others.
5. **Reassignment:** Creator-only. Managers can reassign tasks they created; cannot reassign tasks created by others.
6. **Bulk actions:** Explicitly deferred to Phase 2.
7. **Out-of-office:** Explicitly deferred to Phase 2.
8. **Assignee picker:** Typeahead search over UserProfile (DisplayName + Email), 300ms debounce, active users only.
9. **Inactive users:** Tasks remain; manual reassignment by creator required. Picker excludes inactive users.
10. **UI pattern:** List + detail side panel (desktop), overlay drawer (tablet), full-page (mobile).
11. **Backward compatibility:** `/my/tasks` preserved unchanged for dashboard widget.
